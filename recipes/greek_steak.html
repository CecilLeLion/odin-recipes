Greek Steak

Description

This recipe is inspired from the mediterranean and was seen on Jaime Olivers television show. It has a sweet and savoury taste which is sure to leave you satisfied.

Ingredients:

  • Steak
  • 1 Pomegranate
  • 1/4 cup parsley
  • 1/4 cup mint
  • Lemon zest
  • Lemon juice
  • Crushed pistachios
  • 1/2 yellow bell pepper
  • Handful of mushroom
  • 1/4 red onion
  • 1 cup of rice
  • Olive oil

Steps:

  1. Marinate steak however you like
  2. Cut peppers, onion and mushroom
  3. Put parsley, lemon zest, mint and olive oil into food processor to create a pesto
  4. Prepare pomegranate in bowl
  5. Place rice in boiling water then move off heat, leave covered
  6. Place steak on BBQ, 4.5 minutes per side on bottom rack, then 2.5 mins per side on top rack
  7. Heat pistachios in frying pan until golden, then crush and place in bowl
  8. When water is almost gone from rice add pesto, lemon juice and chopped vegetables, stir and leave covered
  9. When rice is done place on plate, lay steak on top and leave for several minutes for juices to seep into rice
  10. Top with pomegranates and crushed pistachios
  11. Enjoy!
